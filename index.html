Welcome to the website for Aiden Remme's benefit

On May 15, 2016, at 11:00 A.M. there will be a free-will offering lunch/breakfast benefit and Silent Auction held at the Brewster American Legion sponsored by the Sacred Heart Catholic Church in Brewster. To make this benefit a success we need your help.

Should you donate, your company’s name, address and phone number will be listed with your corresponding Silent Auction form next to your donation. Please share the details of our benefit with anyone in your surrounding community that may be willing to donate to the Silent Auction.

If your donation needs to be picked up, please call Darnel Cuperus at (605) 941-3293 and we will gladly pick up your donation. Your donation can also be mailed to the following address:

Darnel Cuperus
24969 477th Avenue
Garretson, SD 57030
(605) 941-3293

Donations will need to be received by May 6, 2016, to ensure we receive them prior to the benefit. Please include a business card along with the enclosed Silent Auction Donation Form with your donation.

Thank you very much for your support and please know that any donation is greatly appreciated. Also, please join us for Aiden’s benefit on May 15, 2016.

Thank you!
